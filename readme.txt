-------------------------------------------------------
    Features
-------------------------------------------------------

Business Blog includes the following features:

    * Recent Posts slider
    * Custom logo
    * Custom colors
    * 2 layouts
    * Show/Hide display controls
    * Social media icons
    * Show full posts or excerpts
    * Custom excerpt length

Business Blog also has a premium plugin upgrade called Business Blog Pro that includes the following features:

    * Custom colors
    * Featured sliders
    * Featured videos
    * Additional layouts
    * Flexible header image
    * Google fonts
    * More widget areas
    * Display controls
    * Custom footer text

Business Blog Pro: https://www.competethemes.com/startup-blog-pro/

-------------------------------------------------------
    Additional Info:
-------------------------------------------------------

    * All menus in Business Blog support 1-3 tiers
    * Featured Images have a 2x1 ratio at all screen sizes
